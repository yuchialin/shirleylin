About

Hey, I'm Shirley Lin :)

Master student pursuing Human-Computer Interaction in the School of Information at University of Michigan. Currently interning at Microsoft Edge Team as User Experience Intern

I was born and raised in Taiwan and moved to a boarding school in Pennsylvania, the United States at 16 years old to continue my education. I completed by bachelor degree in Communication at the University of Washington at Seattle in 2015. After graduation, I moved back to Taiwan and worked as a UX Designer at Learning Digital, a tech firm that develops Learning Management System (LMS) for enterprises.

On a personal level, I am also an avid backpacker; I took my first solo trip in 2013 to Poland; since then I have traveled to 15 countries and visited 30 cities. I love traveling because it allows me to jump into the map and learn the history and nature like nothing else.
